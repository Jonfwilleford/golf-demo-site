Where Tradition Meets the Fairway

Welcome

A Classic Course, A Timeless Experience

Henderson Country Club carries the kind of quiet history most modern courses would pay good money to fake. A classic 9-hole layout stretching 6,071 yards from the longest tees, it plays to a par 71 and has been part of the Henderson, Texas landscape since 1932.

The course offers a relaxed but honest round of golf—the kind where the trees remember your swing and the fairways don't particularly care about your excuses.
